Challenges

Following challenges were the reasons which enforces us to bring a drop off pick up
service for the children and corporate workers

Tme Consuming Journey

Drivers pickup the children and make them sit for an hour in their vehicle until the last child has been picked up

Dangerous Journey

Vans are overloaded by the drivers due to earn more money and children lives are put to threat

Unsafe and Unreliable

Vehicles are mostly not according to the standards and drivers are not fully trained

Solution

Reliazing the mentioned problems and precious lives of the people
we are having a solution which might solve the issues upto a great extent

Area specific Drivers

We are assigning a driver to a particular area so that vehicles can be utilized by limited number of people of nearby areas according to their comfort

Resource and Time Utilization

In their free hours, Drivers and vehicles will be utilized to earn more money by assigning them the employees/workers of the companies and corporate sectors.

Trained drivers with tested vehicles

Only those vehicles will be allowed to be used who are according to standards to ensure safety to the peple

About Us

Want to know..How we started..?

My-DOPU is a startup initiated by a group of individuals belonging to diverse background who strive to solve the transportation problem thrugh technology. We believe that current tranport system for school going children is unsafe and unreliable. Drivers are not getting good income. Vans are overloaded threatning childrens' lives. They spare a lot of time in long journey of vans due to which they become tired in school. So we have come up with the solution of "My DOPU", a Drop Off Pickup Service, who is determined to solve this challenge. Children safety are our top priority.
